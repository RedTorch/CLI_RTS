CONTROLS:
All drones and other objects can be controlled using a sequence of commands, in the format: target_object desired_action parameter_1 parameter_2 parameter_3. For example, typing "drone1 move 100 300" will make drone1 navigate to the coordinates (100,300) on the map.

This "game" is still in development. The next update will probably add enemies with interesting behavior and/or resource collection, and the one after that will be objectives and story, which imo is the most important aspect of the game concept but also the most lacking atm.
